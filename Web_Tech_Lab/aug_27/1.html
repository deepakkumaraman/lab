NITP logo

NITP logo is inspired by sun because Bihar's most famous festival is Chhat puja. Lets see the color scheme of NITP logo. The Department of Computer Science and Engineering offers courses leading to Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering. The department has a very good infrastructure and faculty to provide excellent education. The curriculum is updated regularly to keep up with the growing demands and the changing trends of the software industry and research laboratories.
